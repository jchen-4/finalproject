Diversity and Inclusion
We believe it is our responsibility to get involved with the latest social issues.
Creating a work environment that welcomes people of diverse backgrounds is one of our top goals. However, it's important to go beyond that and bring about real change in our society. We strive to commmit our resources towards advocacy and reform.
We take our public image very seriously. Here are some other companies who have inspired us to dedicate our resources towards great causes.
    In August 2019, two back-to-back mass shootings resulted in the deaths of 30 people. Walmart took the first step and banned open carry in their stores. They received national praise for their dedication to making the country safer.
    Unfortunately, a year later, Walmart reversed its decision and returned the guns and ammunitation back to the sales floor.
    Microsoft has contributed a large portion of their facial recognition software towards the Domain Awareness System, which was originally created for the New York Police Department. In 2020, they decided to ban the use of their software for policing, due to ethical concerns surrounding the technology's unjust treatment of people of color.
    GitHub, owned by Microsoft, continues to renew its contract with ICE.
    Amazon, like Microsoft, is one of the biggest suppliers of facial recgonition technology. In solidarity with Microsoft, Amazon also decided to withdraw its facial recognition software.
    However, its Ring doorbells are still linked with many local police departments.
